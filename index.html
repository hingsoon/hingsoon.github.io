Singapore Round Island Route

In early 2012, the Singapore government announced that it would be building a 150km long Singapore Round Island Route that would run along the coast connecting parks and places of interest.

Objective

This purpose of this map is to give a best guess effort on recreating the Round Island Route from sources available in the web. By using this map, readers can see what is available currently and plan their exploratory routes accordingly The reason that the mapping was difficult is because only high level (low details) graphical maps were found online. Most of the route in this map is based on a best guess effort from graphic files online, from some park connector layer that was made available by the Singapore NParks and the authors knowledge of the area.
